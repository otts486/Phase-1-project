    Slay a Shagaru Magala

    The form taken by Gore Magala once they mature and molt. Gorgeous, glittering scales cover Shagaru Magala, and their divine wings are large enough to wrap around their entire body. According to records, one was once said to have scattered its black scales over an area the size of a mountain, annihilating any and all lifeforms inhabiting that region.

    General Hunting Tips

    Shagaru Magala use a variety of attacks—including area breath attacks and explosive pollen—and wield their black scales to even deadlier effect than Gore Magala. The Frenzy is as much of a threat as ever, so don't forget to bring Nulberries. Keep your eyes and ears peeled for powerful wingarm strikes and their bevy of breath attacks.
