    Elgado Outpost

    Welcome to the Elgado Outpost hunter. Faraway from the rural ninja village of Kamura, the outpost is part of the kingdom built to monitor the crater which recently reopened causing monsters to run amok. This staunch Military outpost will serve as your home base as you investigate the strange happenings. Feel free to acquaint yourself with the facilities.
